Games

For individual pratice, there are spelling games and word games.

Spelling Games

The basic spelling games have four modes: easy, medium, hard and extreme. The modes control how much help a player gets during the game.

Word Games

If you like word searches and scrabble, these could be for you.

Hive Game

A hive games is designed to play in class where children effectively take a spelling test on computers, driven by the teachers computer showing words on the interactive whiteboard.
