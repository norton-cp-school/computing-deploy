Advanced Totals

In this session, we are going to use even more formulas.

When Mr Myers looks at catalogues of equipment, the prices are often given per item. If he wants 20 hoops, he has to multiply the price of single hoop by 20 before he can put it in his list. If the prices change and/or Mr Myers changes his mind about how many to buy, the whole list will need recalculating.

The spreadsheet can help with this too.
