Size Mystery

We have seen that Brighton is far bigger than both Tenby and Happisburgh. Why did it grow so much bigger?

Seaside Holidays

An important reason is simply that people in the 1800s began to enjoy seaside holidays. Tourists travelled from inland towns and cities to swim in the sea and enjoy nice beaches.

Both Brighton and Tenby became important holiday destinations.
